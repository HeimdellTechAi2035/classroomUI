# Week 4 Day 4 Slide 5: Practice Exercise - Pitch Development
**Time: 25 minutes**

## Exercise
Prepare a brief pitch for your innovation idea

## Pitch Structure
1. **Problem**: What issue are you solving?
2. **Solution**: What's your approach?
3. **Benefits**: Why does this matter?
4. **Next Steps**: What would you do first?

## Individual Task
Create and practice your 2-minute pitch
