# Week 4 Day 4 Slide 2: Innovation Challenge Introduction
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Today's Challenge
Design a solution to a real-world problem using all Week 4 skills

## Process
1. **Identify**: Use critical thinking to understand the problem
2. **Generate**: Use creativity to brainstorm solutions
3. **Evaluate**: Use decision-making to select the best approach
4. **Plan**: Outline how to implement your idea

## Challenge Options
- Workplace efficiency improvement
- Community problem solution
- Personal productivity enhancement
