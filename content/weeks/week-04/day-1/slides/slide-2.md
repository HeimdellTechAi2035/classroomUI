# Week 4 Day 1 Slide 2: What is Critical Thinking?
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Core Content
- Analyzing information objectively
- Questioning assumptions
- Evaluating evidence
- Drawing logical conclusions

## Key Concepts
- **Analysis**: Breaking down complex information
- **Evaluation**: Assessing quality and reliability
- **Inference**: Drawing conclusions from evidence
- **Explanation**: Communicating reasoning clearly

## Discussion Points
- Why is critical thinking important in the workplace?
- How do we distinguish facts from opinions?
