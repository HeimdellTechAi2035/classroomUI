# Week 4 Day 1 Slide 5: Practice Exercise - Information Analysis
**Time: 25 minutes**

## Exercise Overview
Analyze a short article or scenario using critical thinking questions

## The 5 Ws + H Framework
1. **Who** is the source?
2. **What** are they claiming?
3. **When** was this created?
4. **Where** does the information come from?
5. **Why** might they present it this way?
6. **How** can we verify this?

## Individual Task
Apply this framework to the provided scenario
