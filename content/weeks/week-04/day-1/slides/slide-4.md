# Week 4 Day 1 Slide 4: Group Discussion - Questioning Skills
**Time: 25 minutes**

## Discussion Prompts
- What questions help you understand something better?
- How do you know when information is reliable?
- What barriers stop us from thinking critically?

## Facilitation Tips
- Encourage all perspectives
- Model good questioning techniques
- Avoid judgment of responses

## Key Takeaway
Good questions are more valuable than quick answers
