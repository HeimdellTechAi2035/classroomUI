# Week 4: Critical Thinking & Creativity

## Overview
This week focuses on developing analytical and creative problem-solving skills essential for the modern workplace.

## Daily Topics
- **Day 1**: Critical Thinking - Analyzing information, evaluating sources, logical reasoning
- **Day 2**: Creative Problem Solving - Brainstorming, lateral thinking, innovation mindset
- **Day 3**: Decision Making - Decision frameworks, risk assessment, confident choices
- **Day 4**: Innovation Workshop - Applying all skills in practical innovation exercises

## Learning Outcomes
By the end of this week, participants will be able to:
- Analyze information critically and objectively
- Generate creative solutions to problems
- Make informed decisions using structured frameworks
- Apply innovative thinking to real-world scenarios

## Time Guide
Each day runs for approximately 2 hours with built-in breaks.
