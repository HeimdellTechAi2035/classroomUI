# Week 4 Day 2 Slide 2: Creative Problem Solving Techniques
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Core Techniques
- **Brainstorming**: Generate many ideas without judgment
- **Mind Mapping**: Visual connections between concepts
- **Reverse Thinking**: Start from the solution and work backwards
- **SCAMPER**: Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other uses, Eliminate, Reverse

## Overcoming Mental Blocks
- Challenge assumptions
- Change your perspective
- Take breaks to allow subconscious processing
- Collaborate with others
