# Week 4 Day 2 Slide 5: Practice Exercise - Mind Mapping
**Time: 25 minutes**

## Exercise
Create a mind map for a problem or goal you're working on

## Steps
1. Write your central topic in the middle
2. Branch out with main themes
3. Add sub-branches with details
4. Use colours and images if helpful
5. Look for unexpected connections

## Individual Work
Apply mind mapping to a personal or professional challenge
