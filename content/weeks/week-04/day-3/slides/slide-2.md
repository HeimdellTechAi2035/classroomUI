# Week 4 Day 3 Slide 2: Decision Making Frameworks
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Core Frameworks
- **Pros and Cons**: Simple but effective listing
- **Decision Matrix**: Weighted criteria scoring
- **Cost-Benefit Analysis**: Comparing value vs investment
- **SWOT**: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats

## Key Principles
- Gather sufficient information
- Consider multiple options
- Think about short and long-term impacts
- Be aware of biases
