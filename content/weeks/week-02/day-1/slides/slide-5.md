**Time: 25 minutes**

## Exercise Type
**Private reflection**: participants assess current skills and identify development priorities.

## Reflection Prompts

### Current Skills Inventory
- Communication strengths you already have
- Problem-solving approaches that work for you
- Time management strategies you use
- Relationship-building skills you possess

### Development Priorities
- Which skills would most benefit your goals?
- What specific aspects need strengthening?
- Where do you feel most/least confident?
- What would skill improvement look like for you?

### Learning Preferences
- How do you learn new skills best?
- What support helps your skill development?
- What barriers have you encountered before?

## Trainer Instructions
- Circulate quietly, available for questions
- Emphasize strengths-based assessment
- Encourage realistic self-evaluation
- Support different reflection methods

## Trainer Note
Focus on existing capabilities as foundation for development.

## Key Message
Effective skill development builds on existing strengths and addresses specific needs.
