## Discussion Prompts
May include:
- Which skills feel most relevant for your goals?
- What skill development has worked for you before?
- What makes skill practice feel safe vs challenging?

## Facilitation Guidelines
### Creating Safety
- Emphasize different skill needs are valid
- Validate existing capabilities
- Normalize learning pace differences
- Respect comfort levels with sharing

### Managing Content
- Focus on individual priorities
- Avoid comparison between participants
- Encourage realistic goal-setting

## Safeguarding Note
Monitor for self-criticism or impostor feelings about skill levels.

## Key Messages
- Everyone brings existing skills and experience
- Skill development is individual and ongoing
- Practice environments should feel supportive
- Small steps lead to significant progress

## Time Structure
- 3 minutes: Discussion guidelines
- 18 minutes: Open sharing
- 4 minutes: Theme summary and transition
