# Week 2 Day 1 Slide 2: Practical Skills Foundation
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Core Content

### Skills vs Knowledge
Understanding the difference between knowing and doing in workplace contexts.

### Foundation Skill Areas
- **Communication skills**: Clear, professional expression
- **Problem-solving approaches**: Structured thinking methods
- **Time management basics**: Priority setting and scheduling
- **Professional relationships**: Collaboration and networking

### Application Focus
Moving from understanding to practice in real workplace scenarios.

## Key Message
Skills develop through practice and application, not just understanding.

## Teaching Approach
- Use workplace examples
- Provide practical frameworks
- Encourage experimentation
- Support individual learning styles

## Learning Outcomes
- Understand practical skill development
- Identify personal skill priorities
- Learn structured practice approaches
- Develop confidence in skill application
