# Week 2 Day 3 Slide 4: Problem-Solving Discussion
**Time: 25 minutes**

## Discussion Topics

### Personal Problem-Solving Experiences
- Share a recent problem you solved (from break reflection)
- What approach did you naturally use?
- What worked well, what was challenging?

### Workplace Problem-Solving Scenarios
**Scenario 1**: A customer complaint about poor service
**Scenario 2**: Team member consistently arriving late
**Scenario 3**: Technology system keeps crashing
**Scenario 4**: Budget cuts affecting your department

### Key Discussion Questions
- Which step of the 5-step model would be most important here?
- What information would you need to gather?
- What are some possible solutions you might consider?
- How would you evaluate which solution to try first?

## Facilitation Approach
- Use small groups (3-4 people) then share back
- Encourage multiple perspectives on each scenario
- Focus on process rather than "right" answers
- Support quieter learners to contribute

## Learning Focus
- Apply framework to realistic situations
- Practice critical thinking together
- Build confidence through discussion
- Learn from different approaches

## Trainer Notes
- Circulate between groups and support discussion
- Be ready with follow-up questions
- Capture key insights on flipchart or board
- Validate all contributions positively
