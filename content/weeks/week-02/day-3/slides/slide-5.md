# Week 2 Day 3 Slide 5: Problem-Solving Practice Exercise
**Time: 25 minutes**

## Exercise: The Missing Delivery Challenge

### Background Scenario
You work in customer service. A regular customer ordered important equipment for tomorrow's presentation, but the delivery hasn't arrived. The customer is calling frantically saying their job depends on having this equipment. The supplier says the delivery left yesterday but has no tracking information.

### Your Task
Use the 5-step problem-solving model to work through this challenge:

1. **Define the Problem** (3 minutes)
   - Write exactly what needs to be solved
   - Identify who is affected and how

2. **Gather Information** (5 minutes)
   - What do you know?
   - What questions would you ask?
   - Who could you contact?

3. **Generate Options** (7 minutes)
   - Brainstorm at least 5 possible solutions
   - Don't evaluate yet - just generate ideas

4. **Evaluate Options** (7 minutes)
   - List pros and cons for your top 3 options
   - Consider customer impact, cost, time, feasibility

5. **Implementation Plan** (3 minutes)
   - Choose your preferred solution
   - Outline first steps you would take

## Work Format
- Individual work first (15 minutes)
- Pair sharing (10 minutes)

## Key Learning
