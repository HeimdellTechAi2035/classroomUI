# Week 2 Day 3 Slide 1: Welcome & Problem-Solving Focus
**Time: 10 minutes**

## Purpose
Welcome learners to problem-solving and critical thinking development.

## Trainer Script
"Today we explore how to approach challenges systematically and think critically about solutions."

## Energiser Activity
Quick problem challenge: "How many uses can you think of for a paperclip?" (2-3 minutes per person, share best ideas)

## Key Focus
- Developing structured problem-solving approaches
- Building critical thinking skills
- Learning to analyze situations objectively
- Gaining confidence in decision-making

## Trainer Notes
- Encourage creative thinking from the start
- Establish safe environment for sharing ideas
- Connect to workplace challenges they might face
- Note different thinking styles in the group

## Time Breakdown
- 2 minutes: Welcome and day introduction
- 6 minutes: Paperclip problem energiser
- 2 minutes: Learning objectives and agenda overview

## Safeguarding Note
Some learners may feel anxious about "being tested" on thinking skills. Reassure that all thinking approaches are valid.
