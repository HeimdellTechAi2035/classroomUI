# Week 2 Day 3 Slide 2: Problem-Solving Framework
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Core Content

### The 5-Step Problem-Solving Model
1. **Define the Problem**: What exactly are we trying to solve?
2. **Gather Information**: What do we know and what do we need to find out?
3. **Generate Options**: What are our possible solutions?
4. **Evaluate Options**: What are the pros and cons of each?
5. **Implement & Review**: Take action and assess the results

### Critical Thinking Skills
- **Analysis**: Breaking down complex information
- **Evaluation**: Assessing evidence and arguments
- **Synthesis**: Combining ideas to form new understanding
- **Reflection**: Considering our own thinking processes

### Common Problem-Solving Pitfalls
- Jumping to conclusions too quickly
- Focusing only on obvious solutions
- Not gathering enough information
- Avoiding difficult decisions

## Key Message
Good problem-solving combines structured thinking with creativity and careful evaluation.

## Teaching Approach
- Present framework step-by-step with examples
- Use workplace scenarios throughout
- Encourage questions and discussion
- Practice with progressively complex problems

## Learning Outcomes
- Understand systematic problem-solving approach
- Develop critical thinking awareness
- Practice structured analysis
- Build confidence in tackling challenges
