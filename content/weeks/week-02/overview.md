# Week 2: Skills Development Week

This week focuses on building practical, applicable skills that participants can use in workplace contexts. Building on the foundations established in Week 1, we move into hands-on skill development across key professional areas.

## Week Overview

**Duration:** 4 days  
**Format:** Interactive workshops with practice sessions  
**Focus:** Practical skill application and development

## Daily Structure

### Day 1: Skills Foundation
- **Focus:** Building practical skills foundation and assessment
- **Key Activities:** Skills inventory, development planning, practice framework
- **Outcomes:** Individual skill priorities identified, foundation established

### Day 2: Communication Skills
- **Focus:** Professional communication framework and practice
- **Key Activities:** Communication scenarios, feedback practice, style development
- **Outcomes:** Enhanced workplace communication confidence and capability

### Day 3: Problem-Solving & Critical Thinking
- **Focus:** Structured problem-solving and analytical thinking
- **Key Activities:** Problem-solving framework, critical thinking exercises, case studies
- **Outcomes:** Systematic approach to workplace challenges

### Day 4: Time Management & Organization
- **Focus:** Time management techniques and organizational skills
- **Key Activities:** Planning methods, priority setting, productivity systems
- **Outcomes:** Personal time management and organization strategy

## Learning Objectives

By the end of Week 2, participants will:
- Have assessed their current skills and identified development priorities
- Demonstrate improved professional communication skills
- Apply structured problem-solving approaches
- Implement effective time management and organizational techniques
- Feel confident applying new skills in workplace contexts

## Assessment Approach

- **Continued focus on understanding over testing**
- **Skills practice in supportive environment**
- **Individual progress recognition**
- **Practical application emphasis**
- **Peer learning and feedback**

## Resources and Support

- Skills assessment tools and frameworks
- Practice scenarios and case studies
- Individual coaching and feedback
- Peer learning opportunities
- Resource library for continued development
