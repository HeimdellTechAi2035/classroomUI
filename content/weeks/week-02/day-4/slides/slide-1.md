# Week 2 Day 4 Slide 1: Welcome & Time Management Focus
**Time: 10 minutes**

## Purpose
Welcome learners to time management and organization skills development.

## Trainer Script
"Today we'll explore practical strategies for managing your time effectively and staying organized in work and life."

## Energiser Activity
Time estimation challenge: "Without looking at clocks, estimate when exactly 30 seconds have passed." Then discuss: How accurate were we? What affects our perception of time?

## Key Focus
- Understanding personal time management styles
- Learning practical organization systems
- Developing priority-setting skills
- Creating sustainable routines

## Trainer Notes
- Many learners struggle with time management due to various factors
- Be sensitive to different work situations and life circumstances
- Focus on practical, adaptable strategies
- Acknowledge that perfect time management isn't realistic

## Time Breakdown
- 2 minutes: Welcome and day introduction
- 6 minutes: Time estimation energiser and discussion
- 2 minutes: Learning objectives and benefits overview

## Safeguarding Note
Some learners may feel shame about past time management difficulties. Create supportive environment focused on improvement, not judgment.
