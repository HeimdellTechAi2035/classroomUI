# Week 2 Day 4 Slide 5: Personal Time Management Plan
**Time: 25 minutes**

## Exercise: Create Your Personal Time Management System

### Part 1: Current State Assessment (5 minutes)
Rate yourself (1-5 scale) on:
- Planning ahead effectively
- Staying organized
- Managing priorities
- Meeting deadlines
- Managing interruptions
- Maintaining work-life balance

### Part 2: Priority Matrix Exercise (8 minutes)
Using the Eisenhower Matrix, categorize your current tasks/responsibilities:

| Urgent & Important | Important but Not Urgent |
|-------------------|-------------------------|
| (Do First)        | (Schedule)              |
| Urgent but Not Important | Neither Urgent nor Important |
| (Delegate if possible) | (Eliminate) |

### Part 3: Personal System Design (10 minutes)
Choose tools and strategies for each pillar:

**Planning & Prioritization**
- Weekly planning time: When? How long?
- Daily planning method: Paper? Digital? When?
- Priority system: ABC? Numbers? Colors?

**Organization**
- Workspace organization: What changes needed?
- Digital file system: How will you organize?
- Information capture: Notes, ideas, tasks?

**Task Management**
- Task breakdown method: How will you handle big projects?
- Batching opportunities: What similar tasks can you group?
- 2-minute rule: How will you apply this?

**Boundaries**
- Time for focused work: When are you most productive?
- Communication limits: Email checking frequency?
- Saying no: What criteria will you use?

### Part 4: Implementation Plan (2 minutes)
Choose ONE thing to implement this week. What will you start with?
