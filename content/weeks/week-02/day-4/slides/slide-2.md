# Week 2 Day 4 Slide 2: Time Management & Organization Framework
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Core Content

### Understanding Time Management
- **Time is finite**: We all have the same 24 hours
- **Energy management**: Working with your natural rhythms
- **Priority-based approach**: Focus on what matters most
- **Systems over perfection**: Consistent habits beat perfect planning

### The Four Pillars of Effective Time Management

#### 1. Planning & Prioritization
- **Eisenhower Matrix**: Urgent vs Important categorization
- **ABC Priority System**: A=Must do, B=Should do, C=Could do
- **Time blocking**: Allocating specific time slots for activities

#### 2. Organization Systems
- **Physical organization**: Workspace, documents, tools
- **Digital organization**: Files, emails, apps
- **Information management**: Note-taking, reference systems

#### 3. Task Management
- **Breaking down large projects**: Manageable chunks
- **Batch processing**: Grouping similar activities
- **The 2-minute rule**: Do it now if it takes less than 2 minutes

#### 4. Boundaries & Balance
- **Saying no effectively**: Protecting your priorities
- **Delegation**: When and how to ask for help
- **Work-life integration**: Sustainable approaches

## Key Message
Effective time management is about making conscious choices that align with your priorities and energy levels.

## Teaching Approach
- Use personal examples throughout
- Provide multiple system options
- Encourage experimentation
- Focus on gradual improvement
