# Week 2 Day 4 Slide 3: Break Time
**Time: 10 minutes**

## Purpose
Provide break and time to mentally process time management concepts.

## Instructions for Learners
- Take a complete break from learning
- Use this break to practice mindful time use
- Notice how you naturally use these 10 minutes
- Refresh and recharge

## Trainer Actions
- Set clear return time and stick to it (modeling time management)
- Be available for questions about concepts covered
- Prepare materials for discussion activity
- Observe how learners naturally manage their break time

## Break Reflection (Optional)
Think about your biggest time management challenge right now. What makes it particularly difficult?

## Environment Check
- Ensure materials are ready for group work
- Check technology and presentation setup
- Organize space for small group discussions
- Review timing for rest of session

## Modeling Note
Use this break to demonstrate good time management - start and end on time, be organized and prepared.

## Accessibility Considerations
Some learners may need extra time to process new concepts. Be available for individual check-ins if needed.
