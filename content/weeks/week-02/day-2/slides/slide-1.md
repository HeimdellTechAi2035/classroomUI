# Week 2 Day 2 Slide 1: Welcome & Communication Focus
**Time: 10 minutes**

## Purpose
Introduce communication skills as practical, learnable capabilities.

## Trainer Script
"Effective communication is a skill set, not a personality trait - it can be learned and improved."

## Energiser Activity
Share one positive communication experience from your work or life (optional).

## Key Focus
- Communication as skill development
- Building on existing communication strengths
- Creating safe practice environment

## Trainer Notes
- Normalize communication challenges
- Emphasize skill-based approach
- Validate different communication styles
- Connect to Week 1 voice and confidence work

## Time Breakdown
- 2 minutes: Welcome and communication focus
- 6 minutes: Positive communication sharing
- 2 minutes: Session objectives and approach
