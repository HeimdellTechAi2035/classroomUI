**Time: 25 minutes**

## Discussion Prompts
May include:
- What communication situations feel most challenging?
- When do you feel most confident communicating?
- What communication skills would benefit your goals most?

## Facilitation Guidelines
### Creating Safety
- Normalize communication difficulties
- Validate different communication styles
- Emphasize learning over perfection
- Respect varying comfort levels

### Managing Content
- Focus on skill development opportunities
- Share practical strategies
- Avoid judgment of communication styles

## Safeguarding Note
Monitor for communication anxiety or past negative experiences affecting confidence.

## Key Messages
- Communication challenges are normal and addressable
- Everyone has communication strengths to build on
- Skills develop through practice and feedback
- Professional communication can be learned

## Time Structure
- 3 minutes: Safety and guidelines
- 18 minutes: Open discussion
- 4 minutes: Key themes and transition
