## Discussion Prompts
May include:
- What makes you feel overwhelmed at work?
- What signs tell you that you need to slow down?
- What boundaries have helped you in the past?

## Facilitation Guidelines
### Creating Safety
- Emphasize voluntary sharing
- Validate all experiences without judgment
- Normalize struggles with boundaries
- Respect different comfort levels

### Managing Content
- Acknowledge work stress is real
- Focus on shared learning
- Watch for signs of distress

## Safeguarding Note
Validate experiences of burnout or pressure. Follow up privately if concerning experiences are shared.

## Key Messages
- Burnout is common, not personal failure
- Boundary-setting skills can be developed
- Different approaches work for different people
- Small changes can have significant impact

## Time Structure
- 3 minutes: Safety guidelines
- 18 minutes: Open discussion
- 4 minutes: Themes and transition
