**Time: 25 minutes**

## Exercise Type
**Private exercise**: participants create a simple pacing plan for themselves.

## Reflection Prompts

### Energy Management
- Ideal working times or energy peaks
- Break frequency that helps
- Signals that indicate a need to pause

### Personal Awareness
- What times of day work best for you?
- How long can you focus before needing rest?
- What type of breaks help you recharge?
- What are your early warning signs of overwhelm?

### Boundary Planning
- What boundaries would be most helpful?
- What makes boundaries hard to maintain?
- What support do you need for boundary-setting?

## Trainer Instructions
- Circulate quietly, available for questions
- Emphasize personal insight, not assessment
- No "right" answers - encourage honest reflection
- Allow different response methods (writing, thinking, drawing)

## Trainer Note
Emphasize flexibility and self-compassion. This is about developing awareness, not creating rigid rules.

## Key Message
Self-awareness is the foundation of sustainable work practices.
