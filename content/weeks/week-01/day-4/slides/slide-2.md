**Time: 40 minutes**

## Core Content

### What are expectations in a work context?
Explain how expectations can become unhealthy when unrealistic or overwhelming.

### Understanding Boundaries
- **Time boundaries**: Working hours and availability
- **Energy boundaries**: Capacity and rest needs
- **Communication boundaries**: Response times and meeting limits
- **Availability boundaries**: When and how you can be reached

### Pacing as a Skill
Working in a way that protects health and focus - consistency over intensity.

## Key Message
Setting boundaries is professional, not a weakness.

## Teaching Approach
- Use practical examples
- Normalize boundary challenges
- Address common guilt around boundaries
- Emphasize professional benefits

## Learning Outcomes
- Understand healthy vs unhealthy expectations
- Recognize boundary-setting as professional skill
- Learn sustainable pacing principles
- Develop personal boundary awareness
