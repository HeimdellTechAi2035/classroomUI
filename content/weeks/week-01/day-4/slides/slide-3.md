# Day 4 Slide 3: Break
**Time: 10 minutes**

## Break Instructions
Encourage participants to move, stretch, and rest eyes.

### Physical Movement
- Stand up and stretch
- Move away from screen
- Gentle movement and breathing

### Eye Rest
- Look away from screens
- Focus on distant objects
- Blink and rest eyes

### Mental Break
- Step away from content
- Allow mind to rest
- Brief mindful moment

## Return Time
Clearly state return time to maintain predictability.

## Trainer Modeling
This break demonstrates sustainable practices - regular rest as essential, not optional.

## Key Message
Breaks are productive tools, not time wasted.
