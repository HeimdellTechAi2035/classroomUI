# Day 4 Slide 1: Welcome & Energiser
**Time: 10 minutes**

## Purpose
Help participants reflect on the week and settle into the session.

## Trainer Script
"Sustainable work is about consistency, not pushing through exhaustion."

## Energiser Activity
Share one thing you learned about yourself this week (optional).

## Key Focus
- Reflecting on Week 1 learning
- Preparing for sustainability focus
- Creating calm, supportive atmosphere

## Trainer Notes
- Emphasize optional participation
- Validate week's challenges
- Set tone for sustainability discussion
- Allow for different energy levels

## Time Breakdown
- 2 minutes: Welcome and settling
- 6 minutes: Reflection sharing (optional)
- 2 minutes: Session overview

"Today's topic - boundaries and pacing - is especially important for people who:
- Have been out of work for a while
- Struggle with anxiety or overwhelm
- Have experienced burnout before
- Tend to say yes to everything
- Feel guilty when they're not working

If any of those resonate with you, know that you're in the right place. These are skills that everyone needs, regardless of background."

---

## Compliance Notes
- Validate all experiences of workplace pressure or burnout
- Watch for signs of perfectionism or self-criticism
- Note any disclosed experiences of workplace exploitation
- Be alert to safeguarding concerns around mental health and stress

## Timing Notes
- 2 minutes: Welcome and sustainable work message
- 6 minutes: Energiser activity and sharing
- 2 minutes: Learning objectives and context setting
