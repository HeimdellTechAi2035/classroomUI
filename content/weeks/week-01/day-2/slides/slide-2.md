# Slide 2: Understanding Assessments
**Time: 40 minutes**

## What Are Assessments?
- Tools to understand individual support needs
- **NOT** tests of intelligence or performance
- **NO** pass or fail scoring
- **NO** ranking or comparison

## Why Do We Use Them?
- To tailor adjustments and pacing
- To provide appropriate support
- To understand learning preferences

## What Information Do We Collect?
- Confidence levels
- Access needs
- Learning preferences
- Support requirements

## Key Message
*Assessments help the programme adapt to YOU*
