# Slide 1: Welcome & Energiser
**Time: 10 minutes**

## Purpose
Settle the group, reduce anxiety, and normalise assessments

## Trainer Script
> "Today is about understanding support needs, not measuring intelligence or performance."

## Activity Instructions
**Quick check-in:** One word for how you're feeling today (optional)

## Trainer Notes
- Create a calm, non-judgmental atmosphere
- Emphasize support over assessment
- Model vulnerability by sharing first if helpful
- Remind everyone that participation is optional
