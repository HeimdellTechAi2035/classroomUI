# RemoteAbility CIC – Training Programme
## Week 1 – Day 1: Welcome & Introduction

**This document contains the complete trainer content, scripts, activities, and compliance notes for delivery of Week 1 – Day 1.**

---

## Session Structure (2 Hours Total)

### 1. Welcome & Energiser (10 minutes)

**Purpose:** Reduce anxiety, establish psychological safety, and create belonging.

**Trainer script:** 
> "Today is not about performance or pressure. It's about arriving safely."

**Activity:** 
- Each participant may share their name and one small hope
- **Important:** Passing is always allowed

---

### 2. Main Learning Activity (40 minutes)

**Key Learning Points:**
- Explain what RemoteAbility is and what it is not
- Reinforce that this programme is supportive, non-competitive, and adaptable
- Explain the 6-week structure and predictable daily format

**Trainer Notes:**
- Focus on creating a safe, non-judgmental environment
- Emphasize that the programme adapts to individual needs
- Clearly outline expectations and reassure participants

---

### 3. Break (10 minutes)

**Instructions:**
- Encourage participants to stretch, hydrate, or step away
- Clearly state the return time to maintain predictability

**Trainer Notes:**
- Use this time to check in with any participants who seem anxious
- Ensure the space remains welcoming for return

---

### 4. Group Discussion (25 minutes)

**Discussion Prompts:**
- What made you decide to join?
- What has been difficult about work previously?
- What do you hope might be different this time?

**⚠️ Safeguarding Note:** 
- Never force sharing
- Follow up privately if distress arises
- Document any concerns discreetly

---

### 5. Practice Exercise (25 minutes)

**Private Reflection Exercise** *(not assessed or collected)*

**Participants reflect on:**
- One difficulty around work or learning
- One personal strength
- One small win from today

**Trainer Notes:**
- Emphasize this is private and will not be shared or assessed
- Circulate quietly to offer support if needed
- Respect participant privacy

---

### 6. Wrap-Up & Preview (10 minutes)

**Key Messages:**
- Reinforce that attending is an achievement
- Preview: Week 1 – Day 2 focuses on initial assessments to understand support needs
- **Emphasize:** There is no pass or fail

**Trainer Reminders:**
- End on a positive, encouraging note
- Provide clear information about next session
- Be available for individual questions

---

## Trainer Audit & Evidence Notes

**Required Documentation:**
- [ ] Attendance recorded
- [ ] Engagement observed (no scoring required)
- [ ] Adjustments requested logged
- [ ] Safeguarding concerns noted privately

**Post-Session Actions:**
- Complete attendance register
- Note any accessibility needs identified
- Follow up on any safeguarding concerns using appropriate channels
- Prepare individual notes for next session based on observations

---

## Resources Required

- **Space:** Comfortable seating arrangement that allows for group discussion
- **Materials:** 
  - Attendance register
  - Private reflection sheets (optional)
  - Programme overview handouts
- **Technology:** None required for this session
- **Refreshments:** Available during break

---

## Accessibility Considerations

- Ensure materials are available in alternative formats if requested
- Allow flexible participation in all activities
- Provide quiet spaces for anyone who needs a break
- Be mindful of different communication preferences and needs
