# Week 6 Day 1 Slide 2: Project Overview
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Project Options
Choose one that showcases your skills:

1. **Skills Portfolio**: Document your key learnings
2. **Problem Solution**: Apply skills to a real challenge
3. **Future Plan**: Career action plan with goals
4. **Creative Presentation**: Demonstrate learning creatively

## Project Requirements
- Shows skills from the programme
- Personal and meaningful to you
- Can be presented in 5-10 minutes
- Demonstrates growth and learning
