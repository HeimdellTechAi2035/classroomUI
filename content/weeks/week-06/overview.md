# Week 6: Project & Graduation

## Overview
The final week brings together all learning through a culminating project and celebration of achievement.

## Daily Topics
- **Day 1**: Project Planning - Choosing projects, planning frameworks, setting goals
- **Day 2**: Project Development - Building projects, overcoming challenges, iterating
- **Day 3**: Presentation Prep - Presentation skills, practice sessions, final refinements
- **Day 4**: Graduation & Next Steps - Final presentations, certificates, future planning

## Learning Outcomes
By the end of this week, participants will have:
- Completed a personal project demonstrating their skills
- Delivered a presentation to the group
- Received their programme completion certificate
- Created a plan for their next steps

## Time Guide
Each day runs for approximately 2 hours with built-in breaks.
Day 4 includes the graduation ceremony and celebration.
