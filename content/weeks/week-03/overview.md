# Week 3: Advanced Skills Week

This week advances participants into leadership, project management, and career planning skills. Building on the communication and problem-solving foundations from previous weeks, we explore more complex professional capabilities.

## Week Overview

**Duration:** 4 days  
**Format:** Advanced workshops with real-world application  
**Focus:** Leadership development and career progression skills

## Daily Structure

### Day 1: Leadership & Influence
- **Focus:** Leadership principles and influence techniques
- **Key Activities:** Leadership style assessment, influence practice, team dynamics
- **Outcomes:** Personal leadership approach and influence skills

### Day 2: Project Management Basics
- **Focus:** Project management fundamentals and tools
- **Key Activities:** Project planning, resource management, timeline development
- **Outcomes:** Basic project management capability and confidence

### Day 3: Digital Skills & Technology
- **Focus:** Digital skills and technology proficiency
- **Key Activities:** Digital tool exploration, technology integration, efficiency techniques
- **Outcomes:** Enhanced digital capability and tech confidence

### Day 4: Career Planning & Next Steps
- **Focus:** Career planning and professional development
- **Key Activities:** Career goal setting, development planning, networking strategies
- **Outcomes:** Clear career direction and development plan

## Learning Objectives

By the end of Week 3, participants will:
- Understand their leadership style and influence capabilities
- Apply basic project management principles and tools
- Demonstrate improved digital skills and technology confidence
- Have developed a clear career plan and next steps strategy
- Feel prepared for advanced professional challenges

## Assessment Approach

- **Advanced skills practice in realistic scenarios**
- **Project-based learning and application**
- **Leadership and collaboration assessment**
- **Career planning and goal-setting focus**
- **Preparation for program completion and next steps**

## Resources and Support

- Leadership assessment tools and development resources
- Project management templates and frameworks
- Digital skills training materials and platforms
- Career planning guides and networking resources
- Advanced coaching and mentoring support
