# Week 3 Day 1 Slide 1: Welcome & Leadership Focus
**Time: 10 minutes**

## Purpose
Welcome learners to Advanced Skills Week and introduce leadership development.

## Trainer Script
"Welcome to Week 3 - Advanced Skills Week! Today we explore leadership and influence - skills that apply regardless of your job title or experience level."

## Energiser Activity
Leadership qualities brainstorm: "Think of someone who influenced you positively - a teacher, manager, family member, or friend. What made them influential? Share one quality." (Create group list of leadership qualities)

## Key Focus
- Leadership as influence, not just position
- Personal leadership style awareness
- Building influence through authenticity
- Developing leadership behaviors anyone can use

## Trainer Notes
- Many learners may not see themselves as "leaders"
- Emphasize that leadership is about influence and behavior, not titles
- Create inclusive environment where all contributions are valued
- Connect to skills from previous weeks

## Time Breakdown
- 2 minutes: Welcome to Advanced Skills Week
- 6 minutes: Leadership qualities energiser
- 2 minutes: Day objectives and leadership mindset introduction

## Safeguarding Note
Some learners may have negative experiences with authority figures. Focus on positive, inclusive leadership models and personal empowerment.
