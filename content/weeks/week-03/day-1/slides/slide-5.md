# Week 3 Day 1 Slide 5: Personal Leadership Action Plan
**Time: 25 minutes**

## Exercise: Develop Your Leadership Growth Plan

### Part 1: Leadership Self-Assessment (8 minutes)
Rate yourself (1-5) on the Five Pillars of Personal Leadership:

**Self-Awareness**
- I understand my strengths and development areas
- I recognize how my emotions affect my behavior
- I actively seek feedback from others
- I have a growth mindset about learning

**Authenticity & Integrity**
- I act consistently with my stated values
- I admit mistakes and learn from them
- Others see me as trustworthy and reliable
- I am genuine in my interactions

**Communication & Connection**
- I listen actively and show empathy
- I communicate clearly and persuasively
- I build positive relationships easily
- I give feedback constructively

**Vision & Purpose**
- I have clear personal goals and values
- I help others see possibilities
- I inspire others through my enthusiasm
- I connect work to meaningful outcomes

**Action & Accountability**
- I take initiative when I see opportunities
- I follow through on my commitments
- I hold myself accountable for results
- I create positive change around me

### Part 2: Leadership Growth Priorities (10 minutes)
- Choose your TOP 2 pillars to develop further
- For each pillar, identify:
  - One specific behavior to start doing
  - One specific behavior to stop doing
  - One specific behavior to continue doing

### Part 3: Influence Action Plan (7 minutes)
Think of one current situation where you want to have more positive influence:
- What's the situation?
- Who do you want to influence?
- What leadership style would work best?
- What specific actions will you take this week?
