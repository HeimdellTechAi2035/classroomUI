# Week 3 Day 1 Slide 2: Leadership & Influence Framework
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Core Content

### Redefining Leadership
- **Leadership vs Management**: Leadership is influence; management is process
- **Leadership without authority**: Influencing peers, inspiring others, taking initiative
- **Personal leadership**: Leading yourself before leading others
- **Situational leadership**: Adapting style to circumstances and people

### The Five Pillars of Personal Leadership

#### 1. Self-Awareness
- Understanding your strengths, values, and impact on others
- Recognizing your emotional responses and triggers
- Knowing your communication style and preferences
- Continuous learning and growth mindset

#### 2. Authenticity & Integrity
- Being genuine and consistent in your actions
- Aligning behavior with stated values
- Building trust through reliability and honesty
- Admitting mistakes and learning publicly

#### 3. Communication & Connection
- Active listening and empathy
- Clear, inspiring communication
- Building relationships across differences
- Giving constructive feedback effectively

#### 4. Vision & Purpose
- Understanding your own purpose and goals
- Helping others see possibilities
- Creating shared vision for teams or projects
- Motivating through meaning, not just tasks

#### 5. Action & Accountability
- Taking initiative when you see opportunities
- Following through on commitments
- Holding yourself and others accountable kindly
- Creating positive change through consistent action

## Key Message
Leadership is about who you are and how you show up, not about your position or title.

## Teaching Approach
- Use diverse leadership examples
- Focus on behaviors, not personalities
- Encourage self-reflection throughout
- Connect to learners' experiences and aspirations
