# Week 3 Day 1 Slide 4: Leadership Styles & Influence Discussion
**Time: 25 minutes**

## Discussion Topics

### Leadership Styles Exploration
**Collaborative Leadership**
- Seeks input and builds consensus
- Strengths: High buy-in, diverse perspectives
- When to use: Complex decisions, team development

**Directive Leadership**
- Provides clear guidance and expectations
- Strengths: Quick decisions, clarity in crisis
- When to use: Urgent situations, new team members

**Servant Leadership**
- Focuses on supporting others' success
- Strengths: High team loyalty, sustainable results
- When to use: Long-term projects, team building

**Inspirational Leadership**
- Motivates through vision and purpose
- Strengths: High engagement, innovation
- When to use: Change situations, creative projects

### Personal Leadership Assessment
In small groups, discuss:
- Which style feels most natural to you?
- Which style do you most respond to in others?
- When might you need to use a different style?
- What leadership behaviors have you already demonstrated?

### Influence Without Authority Scenarios
**Scenario 1**: Convincing colleagues to adopt a new process
**Scenario 2**: Motivating a team member who seems disengaged
**Scenario 3**: Leading a project with no formal authority
**Scenario 4**: Influencing upward to get manager buy-in

## Facilitation Approach
- Start in pairs, then groups of 4, then whole group sharing
- Encourage sharing of real experiences
- Validate all leadership styles as valuable
- Focus on developing range and flexibility

## Learning Focus
- Understand different approaches to leadership
- Identify personal leadership strengths
- Practice applying influence strategies
- Build confidence in leadership abilities
