# Week 3 Day 1 Slide 3: Break Time
**Time: 10 minutes**

## Purpose
Provide break and reflection time on leadership concepts.

## Instructions for Learners
- Take a proper break from learning
- Refresh and recharge for group activities
- Optional: Think about which of the five leadership pillars resonates most with you

## Trainer Actions
- Set clear return time
- Be available for questions about leadership framework
- Prepare materials for discussion activity
- Check in with learners about comfort level with leadership topic

## Break Reflection (Optional)
Consider: "What's one leadership behavior you already demonstrate, even if you don't think of it as leadership?"

## Environment Check
- Set up space for small group discussions
- Ensure materials ready for leadership style activity
- Check technology and presentation setup
- Prepare flipchart paper for group work

## Timing Note
Advanced skills week requires good pacing - maintain schedule to cover all content effectively.

## Accessibility Considerations
Some learners may feel overwhelmed by leadership concepts. Be available to discuss concerns and reinforce that everyone has leadership potential.
