# Week 5 Day 1 Slide 2: CV Essentials
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Key CV Sections
- **Contact Details**: Name, phone, email, location
- **Personal Statement**: Brief summary of who you are
- **Work Experience**: Jobs, roles, achievements
- **Education & Training**: Qualifications, courses
- **Skills**: Both technical and soft skills

## Formatting Tips
- Keep it to 2 pages maximum
- Use clear headings
- Consistent formatting throughout
- No spelling or grammar errors
