# Week 5: Professional Development

## Overview
This week focuses on building professional skills for employment success, from CV building to personal branding.

## Daily Topics
- **Day 1**: CV Building - Creating effective CVs, highlighting strengths, tailoring applications
- **Day 2**: Job Searching - Finding opportunities, using job boards, networking strategies
- **Day 3**: Interview Skills - Interview preparation, common questions, confidence building
- **Day 4**: Personal Branding - Professional identity, online presence, LinkedIn optimization

## Learning Outcomes
By the end of this week, participants will be able to:
- Create an effective, professional CV
- Use multiple job search strategies
- Prepare confidently for interviews
- Build and maintain a professional brand

## Time Guide
Each day runs for approximately 2 hours with built-in breaks.
