# Week 5 Day 2 Slide 2: Job Search Strategies
**Time: 40 minutes**

## Where to Find Jobs
- **Job Boards**: Indeed, LinkedIn, Reed, Totaljobs
- **Company Websites**: Apply directly
- **Recruitment Agencies**: Specialist support
- **Networking**: Word of mouth, connections
- **Social Media**: LinkedIn, Twitter

## Search Tips
- Use specific keywords
- Set up job alerts
- Research companies before applying
- Track your applications
