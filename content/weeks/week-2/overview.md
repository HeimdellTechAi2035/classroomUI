# Week 2: Sales & Communication Training

## Overview
This week focuses on developing essential sales and communication skills. Trainees will learn professional communication techniques and basic sales concepts relevant to remote work.

## Learning Outcomes
By the end of this week, trainees will:
- Demonstrate professional written and verbal communication
- Understand basic sales principles and customer interaction
- Be able to handle common customer scenarios
- Have practiced role-play sales exercises
- Know how to adapt communication style for different audiences

## Session 1: Communication Fundamentals (2 hours)

### Agenda
1. **Week 1 Review & Check-in** (10 mins)
2. **Professional Communication** (35 mins)
   - Written communication best practices
   - Email templates and examples
   - Tone and clarity
3. **Break** (15 mins)
4. **Verbal Communication** (35 mins)
   - Phone/video call etiquette
   - Active listening skills
   - Managing difficult conversations
5. **Practice Activity** (20 mins)
6. **Summary & Homework** (5 mins)

### Trainer Notes
- Use real-world examples from your experience
- Pair trainees for practice activities
- Provide constructive, encouraging feedback
- Watch for anyone struggling with confidence

## Session 2: Sales Basics (2 hours)

### Agenda
1. **Homework Review** (10 mins)
